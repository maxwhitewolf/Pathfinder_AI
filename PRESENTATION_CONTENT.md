# PRESENTATION REPORT CONTENT

## INTRODUCTION

Rapid changes in job markets driven by AI and automation

Students and job seekers struggle to identify suitable career paths

Skill-gap between academia and industry is widening

Need for AI-based systems that provide personalized recommendations

Integrates skills analysis, academic performance evaluation, and intelligent job matching

User-centric system for career awareness, planning, and decision-making

---

## PROBLEM DEFINITION

Lack of personalized guidance for students and early-career professionals

Traditional career counseling is time-consuming and non-scalable

Difficulty in mapping skills/resumes to current industry requirements

No unified platform combining:

- Career recommendations based on skills and academic profile
- Resume parsing and skill extraction
- Skill-gap detection and analysis
- Intelligent job matching using semantic similarity
- Personalized learning roadmaps

Need for AI-driven tools to support career readiness and employability

## LITERATURE REVIEW SUMMARY

Skill-gap analysis and academic profile integration enhances recommendation quality

NLP and embedding models (Doc2Vec) enable semantic job–resume matching

Machine learning models (KNN, contextual bandit algorithms) used for personalized career suggestions

Educational recommender systems increasingly target personalized learning paths

AI models also used for employment tracking and labor market analytics

Resume parsing and automated skill extraction streamline profile creation
